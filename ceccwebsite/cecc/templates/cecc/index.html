Programs for Young Children

Children's Edu-Care centers offer full and part time infant, toddler, and pre-school programs and school age daycare Monday through Friday, 6:00am to 6:30pm. Mid Rivers Day Care offers a large indoor playground and Children's Edu-Care Center is located on a beautiful 3 acre campus with an in-ground pool designed for children.

We offer two child care centers in the Mid Rivers Mall Drive area and both are within two miles of Interstate 70 and four miles of Missouri Highway 94.

We offer the following pre-school and daycare programs led by degreed, experienced teachers:

Mid Rivers Day Care 6:00am - 6:30pm

Cuddle Care

Personal, caring attention infant through two years

Kinder-Prep

Traditional pre-school and PreK three through six years

Children's Edu-Care Center 6:00am - 6:30pm

The Academy

Montessori primary school two and a half through six years

Kindergarten

Preparing for elementary five through six years

Kountry Klub

Before/after school, day off daycare six through twelve years

Kountry Kamp

Summer camp six through twelve years
